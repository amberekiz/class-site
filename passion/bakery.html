BAKING AS MY PASSION.

My love for baking started in middle school when I made my first Oreo cheesecake cookie.

It honestly didn't taste the best (it was way too sweet and it had my friends and I drinking
water after every bite), but despite that, it made me realize the beauty and fun of baking.

Some of the reasons why I enjoy baking:

  • You can eat!
  • Ultimate destresser
  • Room for creativity

But in my opinion, the best part of baking is that its meant to be shared with others. Nothing
beats the feeling than being able to share a meal with others.
